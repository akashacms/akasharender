# 2026-06-24 01:04 - Add Performance Measurement Methodology concept page

Created a new concept page documenting the methodology for performance
measurement and for interpreting performance data in AkashaRender. The
page captures the measure-attribute-interpret-change-re-measure loop, the
distinction between indexing and rendering phases, the use of the
`--perfresults`, `--perf-data-dir`, and `AK_PROFILE_INDEX` mechanisms, the
overlap-ratio and `user`/`real` checks for detecting real parallelism, and
a Risks & Pitfalls section drawn from the lessons of the full techsparx.com
performance review (wrong-bucket optimization, machine-noise vs causal
change, MahafuncArray double counting, the failed pre-read optimization,
and CPU-bound work not benefiting from async concurrency).

The page complements the existing Performance Profiling concept (which
documents the tooling) by documenting the practice of using that tooling.
